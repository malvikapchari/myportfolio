About Me

Hi! My name is Malvika Patrachari. I am nineteen years old and have just finished my sophomore year of college at Rutgers University. I started off my college career in the pre-med track with a major in psychology, but my interests led me to change paths and attempt to pursue a different major, Computer Science. I am still debating a minor, but am leaning towards mathematics.

In my free time, I love spending time with my younger sister (featured in the photo), with friends and with family. Other than that I enjoy practicing Indian classical dance (called Bharatanatyam).

A few years ago, I performed my longest solo Bharatanatyam performance that lasted about three and a half hours. This solo performance is called an arangetram.
